💅 Detailed Treatment Information

💖 Nourishing Clinic Perm

This perm curls your lashes while infusing them with nutrients like protein and keratin, creating healthy, glossy curls without damage.

Main Features:

  • Prevents lash damage
  • Natural curl with a shiny finish

Recommended for:

  • Those with thin or easily damaged lashes
  • Those seeking both curl and nourishment

✨ Basic Strong Curl Perm

Creates dramatic curl from the root to open the eyes beautifully, eliminating the need for an eyelash curler.

Main Features:

  • Strong curling effect
  • Enhances eye definition

Recommended for:

  • Downward-pointing lashes
  • Those who find daily curling bothersome

🐼 Clear Eyes Black Tint Perm

This treatment lifts and curls your lashes while tinting them black for a mascara-like effect without makeup.

Main Features:

  • Curl and tint applied simultaneously
  • Mascara-like appearance

Recommended for:

  • Light-colored or barely visible lashes
  • Those seeking a simplified beauty routine

👀 Dual Perm

This premium perm lifts the roots and softly curls the tips for a defined yet gentle eye impression.

Main Features:

  • Dramatic root lift + soft curl combo
  • Makes lashes appear fuller and longer

Recommended for:

  • Those wanting both definition and softness
  • Those seeking a premium effect

🌿 Brow Lift Perm

Shapes and sets the brow hairs upward, giving the appearance of fuller, well-groomed brows.

Main Features:

  • Aligns and sets brow hair direction
  • Creates the look of thick brows

Recommended for:

  • Unruly or drooping brows
  • Concerns about sparse brow appearance
